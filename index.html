Our Story

Welcome to Lana's Retro Cafe, a dream brought to life by Prem Kumari. Inspired by the nostalgic melodies of Lana Del Rey, our cafe transports you to the golden era of the 60s with its vintage charm and cozy atmosphere.

Located in the beautiful city of Phuentsholing, Bhutan, we specialize in serving classic coffees and small platters that perfectly complement our retro vibe. Our cafe is designed to be a haven for students and anyone looking to relax, with free WiFi and comfortable seating.

Every detail in our cafe, from the music to the decor, is carefully curated to create an experience that feels like stepping into one of Lana's songs - melancholic yet beautiful, nostalgic yet fresh.
